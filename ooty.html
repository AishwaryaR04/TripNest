Ooty

The Queen of the Nilgiris

The Essence of Ooty

Nestled in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu at an altitude of 2,240 metres, Ooty is a breathtaking hill station draped in emerald tea gardens, misty valleys, and pine-scented forests. Known as the Queen of Hill Stations, it enchants visitors with its colonial heritage, toy train rides, and serene lakes surrounded by eucalyptus trees. Life in Ooty is unhurried and restorative. Mornings arrive wrapped in cool mist, evenings glow with golden light over rolling hills, and the cool air carries the gentle fragrance of tea blossoms. Whether you're drawn by the famous Nilgiri Mountain Railway, the vast botanical gardens, or the quiet trails winding through shola forests — Ooty offers a mountain escape that lingers in memory long after you leave.
