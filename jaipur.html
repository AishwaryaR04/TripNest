The Essence of Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is a city where royal grandeur meets vibrant everyday life. Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, the city was built on a grid plan and painted terracotta pink — a colour it still proudly wears. Its magnificent forts, ornate palaces, and bustling bazaars overflow with the colour, craftsmanship, and culture of Rajputana. Every corner of Jaipur tells a story — from the intricate latticed windows of Hawa Mahal catching the morning breeze, to the mirror-studded halls of Amer Fort glittering under candlelight. The city's food, textiles, and architecture are a living heritage that continues to draw travellers from across the world.
